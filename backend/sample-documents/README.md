# Sample Legal Documents for RAG Testing

This directory contains sample PDF documents that can be uploaded to test the RAG system.

## Documents Included:

1. **Business Registration Guide.pdf** - Requirements and procedures for business registration in Nepal
2. **Property Registration Laws.pdf** - Legal procedures for property registration and transfer
3. **Marriage and Divorce Laws.pdf** - Legal procedures for marriage and divorce
4. **Criminal Procedure Code.pdf** - Criminal law procedures and requirements
5. **Labor Laws Nepal.pdf** - Employment and labor law regulations

## How to Use:

1. Upload these PDFs through the admin Documents page
2. Wait for processing to complete
3. Test the chatbot with questions related to these documents

## Sample Questions to Test:

- "What are the requirements for starting a business in Nepal?"
- "What documents do I need for property registration?"
- "What are the legal procedures for divorce?"
- "How do I file a criminal complaint?"
- "What are the minimum wage requirements for employees?"

## Note:

These are sample documents for testing purposes. In production, you would upload real legal documents relevant to your use case.
